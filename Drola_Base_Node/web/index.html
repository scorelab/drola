Authentication steps

Registering the drones before usage is a mandate. For registering and authentication of your drone by the admins, please follow the steps :-

Submit Request

Request needs to be submitted for drone registration along with all personal details and the details of the drone.

Approval

The submitted reques will be verified by the admins and shall be approved, all the details will be stored and usage will be monitored.

Fly your drone

Each drone will be assigned a authentication token by the admins, and then you can fly your drone legally. All your activities shall be monitored.
